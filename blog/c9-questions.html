Asking Questions

Asking questions can save large amounts of time that might otherwise be consumed with fruitless Google searches. It's important, though, to also ask good questions. I feel that I am pretty good at asking questions. I typically just consider my answerer when I formulate my questions. I try to phrase things concisely and specifically. I also try to help people get a clear picture of my question, by helping them to reproduce it (when applicable).
